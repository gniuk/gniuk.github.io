Emacs align-regexp explained in detail with examples

"By gniuk. Date: " 2020-11-18 ". Last updated: " 2020-11-18 "."

Emacs comes with powerful and useful functions to align text to a specific column, by regexp.

align is a simplified align-regexp with some predefined rules applying to current major mode. Sometimes it doesn't work well. So We focus the command align-regexp here.

Example

                #define    A    0x01

                #define  WHAT_EVER  0x1BADB002

                #define      ABCDE     0xabcde
